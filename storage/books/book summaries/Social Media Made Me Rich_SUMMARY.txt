Cialdini’s Principles:
1. **Reciprocity**: Give first (e.g., restaurant mints increase tips 23%)

3. **Authority**: Display credentials (e.g., diplomas in medical offices)
4. **Consistency**: Small commitments lead to big actions (e.g., "Drive Safe" postcards → billboards)
5. **Liking**: Similarity + compliments build rapport
6. **Consensus**: Social proof (e.g., "75% of guests reuse towels")

2. **Competitor Analysis**: Reverse-engineer top-ranking sites
3. **Monetization Check**: Verify affiliate programs (Clickbank, Amazon)
4. **Forum/Social Listening**: Topsy.com + SocialMention.com
5. **Trends**: Google Trends insights
6. **Related Markets**: SEMRush for vertical expansion

**Pro Tip:**  
Target long-tail keywords (e.g., "German shepherd potty training") for less competition.
